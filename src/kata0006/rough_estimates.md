# CodeKata.com Kata 03: How Big? How Fast?

## How Big?

> roughly how many binary digits (bit) are required for the unsigned representation of:
>
> - 1,000
> - 1,000,000
> - 1,000,000,000
> - 1,000,000,000,000
> - 8,000,000,000,000

1,000: 10 bits
1,000,000: 20 bits
1,000,000,000: 30 bits
1,000,000,000: 40 bits
8,000,000,000: 43 bits
